Classical massage is the most popular type of massage, which, depending on form and intensity, is characterized by healing, relaxation and stimulating properties. Classical massage uses all available techniques, such as stroking, rubbing, kneading, staining and vibration. It can be performed with the aid of a treatment such as various types of healing ointments.

DESCRIPTION OF TREATMENT
Classic massage consists in the direct effect of mechanical stimuli on the skin, subcutaneous tissue and muscles. It is done according to the indications, with different movements or grip ne or both hands. It improves circulation, increases tissue elasticity, relaxes the analgesic, increases blood flow to the heart and improves lymphatic circulation.

CONTRAINDICATIONS
All acute and some sub acute inflammation Diseases with high fever (above 38 degrees Celsius) Malignant (malignant and benign) diseases Inflammation of the veins, large varicose veins and aneurysms.
